Campus Resources
  • How do I change my courses?
    Visit your academic advisor first to discuss your option.Then complete the "Change ofMajor" form available at the Registrar's Office. Submit the signed form before the semester deadline.
  • What's the best way to manage my course load?
    Start with a balanced schedule of 12-15 credits in your first semester. Mix challenging courses with easier ones. Use a planner or digital calendar to track assignments and exams.
  • How can I get involved in campus activities?
    Attend the Student Organization Fair during orientation week. Check the campus events calendar regularly. Visit the Student Activities Office for information about clubs and organizations.
  • Where can I find tutoring support?
    The Academic students Bureau offers free tutoring for most subjects. You can also check with your department for subject-specific tutors. Many residence halls also have peer tutoring programs especially in the third semester.
